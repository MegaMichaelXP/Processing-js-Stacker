Michael's Entertainment - Stacker


Controls:
  • Spacebar - Stack blocks/Continue game
  • Z - Claim prize

Gameplay:
  • Stop each level of blocks on top of each other to build the tower.
  • Missed blocks are removed from the next level.
  • If all blocks are missed, the game will end.
  • At the minor prize level, you may take the prize or continue and try for a major prize.
  • No prize is awarded if you continue and miss.

Wagers and rewards:
  • Wager is paid when the game starts.
  • Minor prize awards 2x wager.
  • Major prize awards 5x wager.
